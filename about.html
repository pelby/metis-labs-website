Industrial innovation is our calling

We’re a UK-based startup using AI software to optimise the way millions of household products are manufactured each year.

The result? Less industrial waste, more energy efficiency and more profitability for our customers.  

Since the industrial sector uses ⅓ of the world’s natural resources, we believe this is a really important mission, and we are working with some of the world’s largest manufacturers to make it happen.

We build products that make a positive impact on the world.
